\subsection{Ablation Study}

To validate the contribution of each component in our proposed system, we conduct a comprehensive ablation study on the Town01 dataset (5000 frames). Table~\ref{tab:ablation_full} presents the complete results.

\textbf{Key Findings:} (1) The full system achieves an RMSE of 152.10~m with 335 visual templates and 442 experience map nodes. (2) Removing IMU fusion causes the largest performance degradation (RMSE increases by 15.90~m), indicating its critical role in the system. (3) Removing spatial attention has the smallest impact (RMSE increases by 1.90~m), suggesting relative redundancy or compensatory mechanisms. (4) The full system outperforms the simplified baseline by 7.90~m in RMSE, demonstrating the effectiveness of our bio-inspired design.
